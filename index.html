Z. Richard Rowling is an Australian compositional ambient producer and designer.

His work has been featured on ABC TV, ABC Radio, Rinse FM, and Triple J, amongst others. He has released music through labels and distributors including Etcetc Music (via Universal Music) and Ender Records. He has released three albums and several EPs.
